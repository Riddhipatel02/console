Project in Console



I used Visual Studio to make this project. To get into Visual Studio, you have to download it and follow all the instructions it shows. You can also add the beautify feature and the live server. Then you click "file" then "open folder" and choose a folder.


To open the console screen, you right click and click "inspect" or press Ctrl+Shift+I. Click console. To enter something in the console, you use the tag console.log().
